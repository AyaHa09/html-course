Artificial Intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force in modern society, reshaping industries, influencing decision-making processes, and challenging traditional notions of human capability. One of the most notable applications of AI is in the realm of automation. Industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and customer service have harnessed the power of AI-driven robots and algorithms to streamline operations and increase efficiency. Robotic arms tirelessly assemble intricate components, algorithms optimize supply chain routes, and chatbots provide instant customer support.
